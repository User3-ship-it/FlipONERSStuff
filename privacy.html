Privacy Policy

This game collects minimal user data required for gameplay functionality.

Information we may collect:

  • User ID
  • Username
  • Gameplay data (such as progress and cosmetics)

This information is used only for:

  • Saving player progress
  • Multiplayer functionality
  • In-game purchases
  • Improving the game experience

Our game may use third-party services including:

  • PlayFab (for backend services and player data storage)
  • Photon Engine (for multiplayer networking)

These services may process user data according to their own privacy policies.

We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers.
